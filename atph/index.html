About Us

About Avengers Tech Programming Hub (ATPH) Avengers Tech Programming Hub (ATPH) is a forward-thinking technology training center based in Kano, Nigeria. We are dedicated to empowering the next generation with practical digital and programming skills that meet global standards. Founded with a vision to close the digital divide, ATPH offers a wide range of courses in programming, web development, software engineering, and digital productivity. Our curriculum is designed to equip students — from beginners to advanced learners — with hands-on skills that are directly applicable in today’s job market and tech-driven economy. At ATPH, we believe that technology is a tool for transformation. Our mission is to inspire creativity, encourage innovation, and build a community of problem-solvers who are ready to lead in the digital age. Our training approach emphasizes: Practical learning: Real-life projects and applications Mentorship: Guidance from experienced instructors Inclusivity: Open to all youths regardless of background Empowerment: Supporting entrepreneurship and job-readiness Whether you're a student, graduate, or a tech enthusiast, ATPH provides the platform and support to help you succeed in the world of technology.

Our team of experts is committed to providing the best solutions to streamline your global supply chain.
